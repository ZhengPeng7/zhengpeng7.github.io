Peng Zheng

Email / CV / GitHub / 🤗Hugging Face / Google Scholar

I'm a PhD student in the MHUG group at the University of Trento, advised by Prof. Nicu Sebe, working on Image Segmentation, Object Retrieval, and AI for Biology. Before that, I spent years sequentially at IIAI, MBZUAI, Alibaba, Shanghai AI Lab as research assistant or research intern. I obtained my MSc at the University of Trento and Aalto University through the EIT Digital programme, and my BEng at Hebei Normal University. I'm the inventor and maintainer of the BiRefNet.
